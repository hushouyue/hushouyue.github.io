Policy Visualization for Brax

Google Summer of Code 2025 • PAL-Robotics

Developed the first online Jax-optimized visualizer for Brax, the Reinforcement Learning environment for robotics. Addressed dataflow retrieval challenges in Just-In-Time (JIT) compilation to enable real-time rendering of multi-agent training on GPUs.

GitHub: pal-robotics/brax_training_viewer

Shouyue Hu, Sergi Acosta, Thomas Ung, Óscar Martínez
