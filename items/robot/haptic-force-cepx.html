Force-feedback Gripper for Da Vinci Surgical System

Best Demo Award (1st/14) • JHU Haptics Course • 2025

Developed a real-time haptic grip feedback device for Da Vinci surgical training. Designed a custom Brushless Direct Current direct-drive mechanism and a dual-loop control architecture (200Hz simulation / 1kHz haptic loop) to render realistic soft tissue interactions using a time-varying spring-damper model.
